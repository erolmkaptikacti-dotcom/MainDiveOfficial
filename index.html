Our Story

A Dive With
Soul

The Main Dive isn't trying to be something it's not. We're a neighborhood bar and kitchen built on homemade food, honest prices, and the kind of atmosphere that makes you want to stay for one more round.

Our owners — including Justin, who you might catch handing out hot cocoa at the holiday parade or chatting you up from behind the bar — put their heart into this place every single day. Sauces are homemade. Plates are made fresh. The people here actually care.

Whether you're stopping in after work, catching the Friday fish fry, or hunting down a late-night smash burger, you'll always leave full and satisfied.
